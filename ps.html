Paper Submission

Prospective authors are requested to follow the instructions below for submitting papers to FCEVT - 2020

  • The paper must be written strictly conforming to IEEE FORMAT and not exceeding SIX pages
  • All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference
  • Conference strictly abides to Plagiarism Policy and authors are advised to check plagiarism prior to paper submission
  • Papers must be submitted in PDF/DOC format
  • Each submission will be assigned a PAPER ID. Authors are advised to use this PAPER ID for further correspondences
  • Presentation of an accepted paper at the conference is a requirement of publication in the conference proceedings
  • Authors shall submit their full manuscripts to fcevt2019@gmail.com
