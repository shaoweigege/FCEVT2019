Registration

Registration fees can be paid only after the acceptance of the paper. At least one author of each accepted paper must register at full fee on or before December 20, 2020. The registration fee are as follows:

Cateogory Fee
UG/PG Student Rs.500/-
Full time Research Scholars Rs.750
Academician/Scientist Rs.1000/-
Industry Personnel Rs.1500/-

The registration fee includes conference kit, participation in all the activities of the conference, lunch and snacks. Accommodation for participants will be provided with a nominal fee as per the college norms.
